# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Rula to be a useful technical project for people building local AI, realtime voice agents, and digital humans. Keep discussions focused, evidence-based, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and technical without personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, but back claims with code, logs, measurements, or references.
- Respect privacy, consent, model licenses, and local deployment boundaries.
- Do not post secrets, private infrastructure details, personal voice/face data, or exploit instructions in public threads.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private credentials, private paths, private hostnames, or personal data.
- Encouraging non-consensual voice cloning, face cloning, or identity misuse.
- Using project channels to coordinate abuse or bypass safety controls.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from the project.
